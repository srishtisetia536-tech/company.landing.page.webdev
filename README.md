# 🚀 EduGuard AI – Student Dropout Prediction & Early Intervention System

EduGuard AI is an intelligent system designed to identify students at risk of dropping out and enable early intervention using data-driven insights and machine learning.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Educational institutions often struggle to identify students who are at risk of dropping out.
Traditional methods rely on manual tracking and delayed analysis, which leads to late intervention and reduced student retention.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

EduGuard AI leverages **Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics** to predict dropout risk at an early stage.
This allows institutions to take timely action and support students effectively.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* 🔍 **Risk Prediction**
  Identifies at-risk students using AI/ML models

* ⚡ **Early Intervention**
  Sends alerts to enable proactive support

* 📊 **Data Analytics**
  Provides insights into student performance trends

* 🖥️ **Interactive Dashboard**
  Displays student data in an easy-to-understand format

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

* Machine Learning
* Python
* Data Analytics
* Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. Collect student academic and behavioral data
2. Process data using AI/ML models
3. Predict dropout risk levels
4. Notify institutions for early intervention

---

## 🌍 Project Impact

* Improves student retention rates
* Reduces dropout cases
* Enables data-driven decision making
* Enhances overall educational outcomes
